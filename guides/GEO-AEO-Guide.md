# GEO and AEO Guide for Service Businesses

## Understanding AI Search, GEO, and AEO

Artificial intelligence is changing how customers find businesses online.

Traditional search engines are no longer the only source of information. Customers increasingly receive answers from AI systems such as:

* ChatGPT
* Claude
* Gemini
* Perplexity
* Grok
* Google AI Overviews

Businesses that want to remain visible must optimize not only for search engines, but also for AI-powered answer systems.

---

## What is GEO?

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization.

GEO is the process of helping AI systems understand, reference, recommend, and cite a business.

The goal of GEO is to increase visibility within AI-generated answers and recommendations.

Examples include:

* ChatGPT recommendations
* Claude recommendations
* Gemini responses
* Perplexity citations
* Google AI Overview references

---

## What is AEO?

AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimization.

AEO focuses on creating content that allows search engines and AI systems to provide accurate answers to customer questions.

The goal is not simply ranking for keywords.

The goal is becoming the answer.

---

## How AI Search Differs from Traditional SEO

Traditional SEO focuses on:

* Rankings
* Keywords
* Links
* Search traffic

AI search focuses on:

* Entities
* Relationships
* Authority
* Trustworthiness
* Structured information
* Question-and-answer content

Both remain important.

---

## What Are Entities?

Entities are identifiable things that AI systems can understand.

Examples include:

* Businesses
* People
* Products
* Services
* Industries
* Locations

For example:

CIVADOS is an entity.

Orlando is an entity.

Customer Response Systems are entities.

Industrial Refrigeration Contractors are entities.

AI systems build relationships between these entities.

---

## What Information Helps AI Systems Understand a Business?

AI systems often look for:

* Clear business descriptions
* Service information
* Industry specialization
* Geographic focus
* FAQ content
* Structured data
* Consistent business information
* Public references

---

## Why FAQs Matter

FAQs are one of the most effective forms of AEO content.

FAQs:

* Match customer questions
* Provide direct answers
* Improve AI comprehension
* Improve search understanding

Examples include:

* What is an AI receptionist?
* What is workflow automation?
* What is GEO?
* What is AEO?
* How does missed-call text back work?

---

## Why Structured Data Matters

Structured data helps search engines and AI systems understand:

* Organizations
* Services
* Locations
* FAQs
* Articles
* Reviews

Structured data improves machine readability.

---

## What is AI Visibility?

AI visibility refers to how often a business appears within AI-generated answers.

Examples include:

* ChatGPT recommendations
* Claude responses
* Gemini responses
* Google AI Overviews
* Perplexity citations

## How Businesses Improve AI Visibility

Common strategies include:

### Creating Helpful Content

Publish:

* Guides
* FAQs
* Industry resources
* Educational content

### Building Authority

Demonstrate expertise through:

* Experience
* Industry knowledge
* Consistent publishing

### Improving Entity Recognition

Clearly communicate:

* Who the business is
* What the business does
* Who it serves
* Where it operates

### Maintaining Consistency

Keep business information consistent across:

* Websites
* Directories
* Social profiles
* Knowledge resources

## What is Local SEO?

Local SEO helps businesses become visible in location-based searches.

Examples:

* HVAC contractor near me
* Industrial refrigeration contractor Orlando
* Air compressor repair company near me

Local SEO remains important even as AI search grows.
