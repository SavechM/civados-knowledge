## Summary

CIVADOS helps service businesses improve customer response, visibility, reputation, lead conversion, and operational efficiency through automation systems and AI-assisted workflows.

The company specializes in practical business outcomes rather than technology for its own sake.

CIVADOS primarily serves service businesses, contractors, industrial service companies, and commercial service organizations.
