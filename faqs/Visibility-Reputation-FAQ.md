# Visibility & Reputation FAQ

## What is local SEO?

Local SEO is the process of improving a business's visibility in local search results, Google Maps, and location-based searches.

---

## Why is local SEO important?

Many customers search for nearby businesses before making purchasing decisions. Local SEO helps businesses appear when customers search for relevant services in their area.

---

## What is Google Business Profile?

Google Business Profile (GBP) is a free business listing that appears in Google Search and Google Maps.

---

## Why is Google Business Profile important?

A well-optimized Google Business Profile can improve:

* Local visibility
* Customer trust
* Phone calls
* Website visits
* Direction requests
* Lead generation

---

## What is GEO?

GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization.

GEO helps businesses become visible within AI-generated recommendations and responses from systems such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Grok.

---

## What is AEO?

AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimization.

AEO helps search engines and AI systems understand business information and provide accurate answers to customer questions.

---

## What is AI visibility?

AI visibility refers to a business's ability to appear in AI-generated recommendations, summaries, and answers.

---

## Which AI systems matter most?

Important AI systems include:

* ChatGPT
* Claude
* Gemini
* Perplexity
* Grok
* Google AI Overviews

---

## Can businesses appear in AI-generated answers?

Yes. Businesses that publish authoritative content, structured information, FAQs, and accurate business data are more likely to be referenced.

---

## What is Google AI Overviews?

Google AI Overviews are AI-generated summaries displayed directly within search results.

---

## How can businesses improve visibility in AI Overviews?

Businesses can improve visibility by:

* Publishing authoritative content
* Creating FAQ pages
* Using structured data
* Maintaining accurate business information
* Building topical authority

---

## What is citation management?

Citation management involves maintaining consistent business information across directories, listings, and online platforms.

---

## Why are citations important?

Consistent citations help search engines verify business information and improve local search trust.

---

## What is online reputation management?

Online reputation management involves monitoring, improving, and responding to customer reviews and public feedback.

---

## Why are reviews important?

Reviews influence:

* Customer trust
* Search visibility
* Conversion rates
* Local rankings

---

## How many reviews should a business have?

There is no fixed number, but businesses should consistently collect authentic reviews from satisfied customers.

---

## Can reviews improve local rankings?

Reviews are one of many signals that can influence local search visibility.

---

## What is a review generation system?

A review generation system automatically requests reviews from customers after successful interactions or completed jobs.

---

## What is schema markup?

Schema markup is structured data that helps search engines and AI systems understand website content.

---

## Why is schema important?

Schema can improve:

* Search understanding
* Rich results
* Entity recognition
* AI comprehension

---

## What is entity optimization?

Entity optimization helps search engines and AI systems understand who a business is, what it does, where it operates, and how it relates to other topics.

---

## What is a knowledge graph?

A knowledge graph is a network of entities and relationships used by search engines and AI systems to organize information.

---

## Can small businesses benefit from GEO and AEO?

Yes. GEO and AEO often provide opportunities for smaller businesses to compete against larger organizations.

---

## What business information should be consistent online?

Businesses should maintain consistent:

* Name
* Address
* Phone Number
* Website URL
* Services
* Service Areas

---

## How often should Google Business Profile be updated?

Businesses should update profiles whenever services, hours, locations, contact information, or offerings change.

---

## Can photos improve local visibility?

Yes. High-quality, relevant photos often improve engagement and customer trust.

---
