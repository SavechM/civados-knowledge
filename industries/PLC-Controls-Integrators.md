## Common Customer Questions

### What is a PLC?

A Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) is an industrial computer used to control machinery, manufacturing processes, and automated systems.

### What is SCADA?

SCADA stands for Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition. SCADA systems provide monitoring, control, and data collection for industrial operations.

### What does a Controls Integrator do?

Controls Integrators design, program, implement, and maintain industrial automation systems.

### Why do manufacturers invest in automation?

Manufacturers often invest in automation to improve productivity, consistency, safety, quality, and operational efficiency.

### Can automation reduce downtime?

Yes. Automation systems often improve monitoring, diagnostics, and preventive maintenance capabilities.
