## Common Customer Questions

### What causes industrial pump failures?

Common causes include:

* Bearing failure
* Seal failure
* Misalignment
* Cavitation
* Improper maintenance
* Vibration issues
* Wear and corrosion

### What is cavitation?

Cavitation occurs when vapor bubbles form and collapse within a pump, potentially causing damage and reduced performance.

### Why is preventive maintenance important?

Preventive maintenance helps reduce downtime, improve reliability, extend equipment life, and lower operating costs.

### How often should industrial pumps be serviced?

Service intervals vary based on operating conditions, equipment type, environment, and manufacturer recommendations.

### Can pump failures impact production?

Yes. Pump failures can disrupt production, reduce efficiency, create safety risks, and increase operational costs.
